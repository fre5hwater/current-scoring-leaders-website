How a commission goes.

Six steps from "we have a project" to "files are delivered." Typical Single = 5–10 business days.

1

Brief

You send the project, references, and constraints. We respond with scope, timeline, fee.

2

Spotting

We watch with you and mark every music in/out and hit point.

3

Sketch

First musical pass — melody, harmony, groove. Fast feedback loop.

4

Production

Full instrumentation, layers, dynamics, sync to picture.

5

Mix & Master

Balance, EQ, automation. Mastered to your delivery target (broadcast, streaming, theater).

6

Deliver

WAV + MP3 + stems + alts. License or WFH agreement signed alongside.
