Our Process

From First Email to Final Master — In Five Steps

A clear, predictable workflow built for both indie creators and production teams. No mystery. No drift. Always a real person on the other end.

01
Day 0–2 · Free

Inquiry & Discovery Call

You submit the project inquiry form, send an email, or book a 20-minute discovery call. We respond within 48 hours — from the founder, not a chatbot.

You bring:

  • Project type (game, film, podcast, ad, brand piece)
  • Mood, references, and any draft brief
  • Deadline and rough budget range
  • Anything you're worried about

You leave with: a written reply outlining whether this is a fit, what tier we'd recommend, and a rough quote.

02
Day 2–5 · Scoping

Statement of Work & Deposit

If we move forward, we send a one-page Statement of Work that locks in: deliverables, deadline, license tier, revision rounds, total fee, and deposit. Plain English with the formal terms attached.

You sign electronically. We invoice the deposit (commonly 50%). Once it clears, work begins.

Why a SOW

Because vague verbal deals are how creative projects go sideways. Both sides know what's owed by whom and when, in writing, before any work happens.

03
Days vary · Composition

Sketches & First Delivery

We start with one or more short musical sketches (15–30 seconds) so you can react to the direction before we commit to a full track. Sketches are usually delivered within 3–7 days.

Once you pick a direction, we extend it into the full piece(s). For multi-track engagements (game OST, podcast bed library, brand suite) we typically anchor with the first, last, and a middle track to lock the arc, then fill in.

You get progress updates without having to ask.

04
1–2 weeks · Revisions

Revisions Within Your Package

Each tier includes a fixed number of revision rounds (Catalog: 1; Custom: 2; Premium: 3). Revisions are submitted in writing, consolidated per round — not iterative drip feedback.

  • Round 1: structural feedback (length, tempo, mood, instrumentation)
  • Round 2: arrangement details (transitions, dynamics, mix)
  • Round 3+ (Premium): polish (master, balance, final EQ)

Beyond the included rounds, additional revisions are billed at the SOW rate. Most clients use one or two.

05
Final Day · Delivery

Final Files, License & Registration

You receive a delivery package containing:

  • Master files: WAV 24-bit/48 kHz + MP3 320 kbps
  • Stems (Premium and above): drums, bass, melodic, FX
  • Loop-trimmed versions (where requested)
  • Signed license PDF defining scope, term, territory, exclusivity
  • Authorship statement suitable for E&O underwriting and PRO registration
  • U.S. Copyright Office registration confirmation (Premium and Work-for-Hire)

Final invoice is paid on delivery. The relationship doesn't end there — we stay reachable for license questions, follow-on projects, and renewals.

Typical Turnarounds

Catalog license: same day — pick a track, pay, get the license.

Custom single track: 1–2 weeks from signed SOW.

Premium piece (with stems and registration): 2–3 weeks.

Multi-track package (game OST, podcast bed library): 3–5 weeks.

Faster turnarounds available with rush fees. Slower turnarounds available at no extra charge if your timeline is flexible.
